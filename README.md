# Golden-youth-Empowerment-CBO-
Golden youth Empowerment CBO was founded in the year 2023 in Mombasa Kenya by kerubo Emmah Okioi a.k.a Emmy love.. our main mission is to empower the UNDERPRIVILEGED KIDS in the community and make sure our young mothers would also get another chance to fulfill their dreams through the program called Girl child moulders program.
