About Us


Golden Youth Empowerment CBO is a community-based organization based in Mombasa County, Kenya. We empower young people through education, leadership, mentorship, entrepreneurship, environmental conservation, girl child empowerment, and community outreach.

Our Programs


Girl Child Moulders Program

Mentorship, dignity support and leadership development for girls.

Youth Employment

Training, entrepreneurship and job readiness for young people.

Education Support

School uniforms, books and learning materials for needy children.

Community Outreach

Medical camps, environmental clean-up, food donations and mentorship.

Daycare Project

Safe and affordable childcare services for working families.
